Surrogate Avatar Portal

Welcome to the Surrogate Avatar Portal! Below you can choose between becoming an avatar or requesting one!

What is this program about?

This program allows students who are not able to get to campus for whatever reason have a volunteer go to campus in their place to act as their avatar. By giving your availability and and contact information you can become an avatar! Or you can give the details of when you need an avatar to be matched with possible avatars for you!
