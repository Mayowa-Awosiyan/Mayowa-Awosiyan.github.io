What is the Purpose of this Program?

The purpose of the Surrogate Avatar Experience is to enable individuals to remotely explore and interact with environments and events that they may not be physically present for. This technology is designed to provide a unique and immersive experience, breaking down geographical barriers and allowing you to engage in activities and events from the comfort of your own location.
